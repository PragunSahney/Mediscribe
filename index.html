💡 How to Get Best Results

🗣 During the consultation, mention:

  • Chief complaint + duration ("fever for 3 days")
  • Associated symptoms ("headache, body pain")
  • Vitals aloud ("BP is 130/85, temp 101°F")
  • Examination findings
  • Diagnosis and reasoning
  • Medicine names and doses

🤖 What AI will auto-infer:

  • Standard drug doses if not mentioned
  • Typical advice for the diagnosis
  • Follow-up timeframe
  • ICD-10 relevant diagnosis codes

🌐 Language tip:

  • Select the language before recording
  • Hinglish (mixed) works well for most Indian consultations
